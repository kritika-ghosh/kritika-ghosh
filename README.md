<h3 align="left">🧠 Research Spotlight: Mental Burnout Detection</h3>
<p>
Right now, my flagship research and development focus is my <b>Mental Burnout Detection System</b>. Burnout is a silent epidemic, especially in fast-paced academic and tech environments. 
<br><br>
My goal with this project is to build an intelligent, data-driven system that can identify the early physiological and behavioral markers of burnout before it becomes overwhelming. By leveraging machine learning to analyze these markers, I'm hoping to create a tool that goes beyond just data collection and actually empowers people to take proactive care of their mental health. 
</p>
